Courses Offered

  • Bachelor of Commerce
  • Bachelor of Business Administration
  • Bachelor of Computer Application

Bachelor of Commerce

Our B.Com program provides students with a comprehensive understanding of business, finance, and accounting principles. It prepares them for diverse career opportunities in commerce, banking, financial services, and entrepreneurship, equipping them with practical skills and theoretical knowledge to thrive in the business world.

Bachelor of Business Administration

The BBA program is designed to develop essential managerial and leadership skills, offering students a deep insight into business operations, marketing, human resources, and finance. This program prepares graduates to take on key roles in various industries, paving the way for successful careers in management, consulting, or entrepreneurship.

Bachelor of Computer Application

The BCA program offers students a solid foundation in computer science, programming, and IT. With a focus on practical applications, it prepares students for thriving careers in software development, data analytics, system management, and emerging technologies, making them adaptable to the dynamic IT industry.
