Bachelor of Business Administration

About the Department

St. Anthony's College offers a Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) course, which was launched in 2009 and is affiliated with Bangalore University. This undergraduate program is designed to prepare students for dynamic careers in business management. The curriculum provides a solid foundation in core business and management principles while developing analytical, leadership, and decision-making skills essential for success in today's business environment.

The BBA program at St. Anthony's College emphasizes a holistic approach to education. It integrates theoretical knowledge with practical application through internships, case studies, and industry interactions. The course fosters entrepreneurial thinking, critical problem-solving, and ethical decision-making, equipping students to adapt and thrive in a rapidly evolving global business landscape.

  • To develop managers with inter disciplinary knowledge.

The department currently offers both the National Education Policy (NEP) syllabus introduced in 2021 and the State Education Policy (SEP) syllabus introduced in 2024. The NEP emphasizes inclusivity, holistic education, critical thinking, multilingualism, and skill development. Under this framework, students can choose open elective courses from other departments and complete internships in their final year. The SEP focuses on setting statewide standards for accountability while promoting professional and moral education.

To enhance learning, the Management Club "Chanakya" organized a Product Launch Competition and a Seminar on Brand Management, fostering teamwork, skill enhancement, and healthy competition. Additionally, Tech Brain Tree conducted a session on Personal Branding as an add-on course for BBA students, helping them build professional skills. Impressively, 90% of students have secured placements in reputed organizations.

The faculty has achieved commendable milestones, such as winning Best Research Paper Awards at national conferences and clearing the NET exam. Students have also excelled, winning awards for best paper presentations at national conferences and participating in intercollegiate events like Best Manager and Treasure Hunt competitions, showcasing their talents, knowledge, and leadership skills.
