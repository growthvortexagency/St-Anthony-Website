About the Department

St. Anthony's College, a prominent institution in Bangalore, has been offering a Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com) program since 2010. The program is designed to provide students with a solid foundation in commerce and business principles. It aims to equip students with the knowledge, skills, and competencies required to excel in the dynamic fields of commerce and finance.

With a comprehensive curriculum, experienced faculty, and state-of-the-art facilities, St. Anthony's College ensures that its students are well-prepared to tackle the challenges of the business world and achieve their professional aspirations. Departmental Programs

Chart Your Career (Career Guidance for Aspiring Chartered Accountants)

This session helps students gain a clear understanding of the Chartered Accountancy profession, providing them with the necessary steps and resources to pursue a successful career in accounting. Students gain clarity on career paths, requirements, and opportunities in the field of Chartered Accountancy.

Commerce Forum Inauguration

The inauguration of the Commerce Forum serves as a platform for students to engage in discussions, workshops, and events related to the commerce industry. Encourages active participation, networking, and the exchange of ideas among students and professionals.

HR Session

A dedicated session on Human Resources provides students with insights into HR practices, recruitment processes, and career opportunities in the field. Students develop a deeper understanding of HR functions and the skills required to excel in human resources roles.

Business Quiz

The Business Quiz challenges students' knowledge of current business trends, finance, economics, and industry practices. Enhances students' analytical and problem-solving skills, while promoting healthy competition and teamwork.

Entrepreneurship Session

A session focused on entrepreneurship introduces students to the fundamentals of starting and managing a business. Outcome: Inspires and equips students with the tools and mindset to pursue entrepreneurial ventures and innovation.
